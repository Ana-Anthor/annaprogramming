How to download OS for your Raspberry Pi

What you need

  • Raspberry Pi

  • MicroSD card

  • A PC or Mac

    To download the software

  • A MicroSD Card Reader

    to transfer the software to the MicroSD card

What to do

  1. Raspberry Pi Imager

    Go to raspberrypi.com

    Download Raspberry Pi Imager.

  2. Transfer the image file to the MicroSD card.

    Open Raspberry Pi Imager and select your Raspberry Pi device.

    Choose operating system. If you don't know which one to select, you can most likely choose "Raspberry Pi OS (64-bit)"".

    Select Storage. Here you select your MicroSD card. Remember this will format your card and all data will be lost, so make sure you don't have anything on it that you want to keep.

    Press "Write" and wait to the process has finished.

  3. You can now put your MicroSD card in your Raspberry Pi.
